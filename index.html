Privacy Policy for Focus

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Focus ("the App", "We", "Us", or "Our") handles information when You use the App. Focus is a screen time management tool developed by Damian Kostycz, an independent developer based in Poland.

The most important thing You should know: Focus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information is stored locally on Your device and never leaves it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what Focus does not do:

  • We do not collect Your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not require You to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not track Your location.
  • We do not collect Your IP address.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services.
  • We do not use advertising networks or trackers.
  • We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.
  • We do not transmit any information from Your device to any server.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Focus stores the following information exclusively on Your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults within an App Group container). This information never leaves Your device:

  • Session settings — the apps You choose to allow during focus sessions and the session duration.
  • Session history — start and end times of completed focus sessions, used for displaying Your weekly insights.
  • Override records — timestamps of when You overrode a focus session, used for self-reflection in the insights screen.
  • App preferences — Your last used session duration, configured switch limits, and onboarding completion status.

You can delete all this information at any time by deleting the App from Your device.

3. Apple Screen Time API (FamilyControls)

Focus uses Apple's FamilyControls and ManagedSettings frameworks to enable app blocking during focus sessions. This means:

  • Focus requests Your permission to access Screen Time when You first use the App.
  • When You select apps to allow during a session, Apple's system picker provides Focus with opaque tokens. These tokens cannot be used to identify specific apps outside of the App itself and are not personal information.
  • Focus uses these tokens locally to apply and remove app restrictions through Apple's ManagedSettings API.
  • No information about Your app usage is transmitted to Us or any third party.

The data handled by these Apple frameworks remains entirely within Apple's secure on-device APIs.

4. Notifications and Permissions

Focus may request the following permissions:

  • Screen Time access (required) — to block apps during focus sessions.
  • Notifications (optional) — to alert You when a session ends or is about to end.
  • Face ID / Touch ID (optional) — to confirm session overrides and time extensions.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in Your device's Settings. Revoking permissions may limit some functionality of the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Focus is suitable for users of all ages. Because We do not collect any personal data, no special procedures regarding children's privacy are required. However, the App is designed for self-directed screen time management and is most beneficial for users aged 13 and above. Users under 16 should use the App with parental guidance.

We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 16.

6. Data Security

Because Focus does not transmit data outside Your device, the security of Your information is protected by Apple's iOS security model. This includes:

  • Sandboxing of app data
  • Encryption of device storage when Your device passcode is set
  • Apple's secure storage of Screen Time tokens

We have no servers, no databases, and no access to any information related to Your use of the App.

7. Your Rights

Since We do not collect or store any personal data on our end, there is no data for Us to provide, modify, or delete on Your behalf. You have full control over all data stored locally on Your device:

  • Access — view Your session history within the App's Insights screen.
  • Delete — uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • Modify — change session settings, preferences, or override records directly within the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If We make changes, We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Policy. Because We do not collect contact information, We are unable to notify You of changes directly. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
